Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git can help us keep track of our version history when working on projects

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to sports. For example you could analyze
previous cricket matches to create a machine learning model that can predict
who will win the upcoming cricket match between India and England!


Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging will join both branches together keeping their history
while rebasing will move one branch into another destroying any previous history
